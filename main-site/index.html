Our Amenities

Sleeps 8

Our 8 sleeper, 4 room, 2 bathroom self-catering bush villa is the perfect choice for a comfortable nature getaway, whether it's with friends, with family or your're looking for a romantic getaway, tailor-made packages can be arranged to suite your needs.

Vredefort Dome Mountain Valley

Crater Bush Villa is situated in the heart of Vredefort Dome Mountain Valley. 2,023 million years ago, after a crater impact, the beautiful mountains and valleys were formed, which you get to enjoy today with a rejuvenated view on life.

Unesco Heritage Site

In 2005 the area was declared as a heritage site. It is 1 of only 8 heritage sites in South Africa, so don't miss the chance to experience this rare beauty. It is the oldest astrobleme yet found on Earth. With a radius of 190 km, it is also the largest and the most deeply eroded.

Indoor Braai/Entertainment Area

The indoor braai/entertainment area is a place where many memorable moments can be had around meals with family and friends, or sharing some drinks late into the night while trying your hand at our dart board.

Local Activities

With nearby activities like cycling, hiking, ziplining, fishing, white water rafting, bird & wildlife watching, as well as a nearby petting zoo, you and the family are spoiled for choice with Crater Bush Villa.

Abundant Wildlife

Enjoy a morning coffee, laid back lunch or a cocktail sundowner on the open veranda, with breathtaking views of the surrounding scenery and abundant birds & wildlife. Maybe you'll even get the chance to meet some of our inquisitive monkeys.
